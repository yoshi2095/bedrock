{{ self.page_title() }}

{% trans %} The Internet only stays healthy if we trust it as a safe place – to explore, transact, connect, and create. Our privacy and security online is under constant threat. But there’s something you can do about it: get informed, protect yourself, and make your voice heard. {% endtrans %} {% trans %} A healthy Internet depends on you. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} Here are three areas where we can make a difference. {% endtrans %}

  • {{ _('User Control') }}
  • {{ _('Cyber Security') }}
  • {{ _('Government Surveillance') }}

{{ _('User Control: Deciding who can collect your data') }}

{% trans %} Right now, we all lack meaningful choice online – privacy policies are often miles long and hard to read, we don’t understand what information we’re sharing or when, and opting out is seldom on the menu. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} We should all be able to choose – with clarity and confidence – what information we share with what companies, understanding the tradeoffs we’re making when we do. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} 91% of adults agree that consumers have lost control of how personal information is collected and used by companies. {% endtrans %}

{% trans pew='http://www.pewinternet.org/2015/05/20/americans-attitudes-about-privacy-security-and-surveillance/' %} Source: Pew Research Center, May 2015 {% endtrans %}

{{ _('What you can do about user control') }}

  1. {% trans ios='https://support.apple.com/HT203033', android='https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/6014972' %} Make sure your mobile apps access only the info they need. Control your privacy and location settings on iOS and Android. {% endtrans %}
  2. {% trans google='https://www.google.com/ads/preferences', yahoo='https://info.yahoo.com/privacy/us/yahoo/opt_out/targeting/details.html', facebook='https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=ads&view', acxiom='https://aboutthedata.com/' %} Know your settings. You can manage your profile and preferences for Google, Yahoo! and Facebook ads, and even edit data that’s been collected about you by Acxiom, one of the world’s largest marketing data brokers. {% endtrans %}

{{ _('What Mozilla is doing about user control') }}

  1. {% trans privacy=url('privacy') %} Writing our own privacy policy in clear, understandable language. {% endtrans %}
  2. {% trans focus='https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/firefox-focus-privacy-browser/id1055677337?mt=8' %} Walking the talk with our own products, with features like the Forget Button, and Firefox Focus, our private browser for iOS. {% endtrans %}
  3. {% trans leandata=url('mozorg.about.policy.lean-data') + '?control' %} Encouraging and educating the industry about lean data practices. {% endtrans %}
{# end #control #}

{{ _('Cyber Security: Locking down your sensitive information') }}

{% trans %} At this point, it feels like we’ve all been victims of a cyberattack somewhere, somehow. Data breaches can lay bare the passwords of millions of people, often going undiscovered for years. Which means your identity may be at risk of theft without you even knowing it. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} We should all have the ability to protect our online identity. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} Breaches affected hundreds of millions of accounts in 2013-2016. In December 2016, the biggest breach in history was reported: 1 billion accounts. {% endtrans %}

{% trans wiki='https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_data_breaches' %} Source: Wikipedia, List of data breaches, 2013-2016 {% endtrans %}

{{ _('What you can do about cyber security') }}

  1. {% trans strongpass='https://support.mozilla.org/kb/create-secure-passwords-keep-your-identity-safe', passmanager='https://www.wired.com/2016/01/you-need-a-password-manager/' %} Choose strong, unique pins and passwords, and use a password manager. (Note: we haven’t tried them all – see what works for you.) {% endtrans %}
  2. {% trans twofactor='https://www.lockdownyourlogin.com/' %} An extra step goes a long way. For the best protection, take advantage of 2-factor authentication wherever it’s offered. {% endtrans %}

{{ _('What Mozilla is doing about cyber security') }}

  1. {% trans codemoji='https://advocacy.mozilla.org/encrypt/codemoji/', letsencrypt='https://letsencrypt.org/' %} Educating about the value of encryption, and advocating for its universal adoption. {% endtrans %}
  2. {% trans leandata=url('mozorg.about.policy.lean-data') %} Educating the industry about lean data practices. {% endtrans %}
{# end #cybersecurity #}

{{ _('Government Surveillance: Keeping prying eyes and ears out of your business') }}

{% trans %} You wouldn’t want the government following your every move in real life – there’s no reason they should be shadowing you on the Internet. The Edward Snowden disclosures showed that even democracies can and do take liberties with your privacy. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} We should all have the freedom to be ourselves — online and off – without surveillance, judgment and imposed societal bias. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} Just four in 10 (38%) of internet users trust that their activities on the internet are not being monitored. {% endtrans %}

{# L10n: 'Centre for International Governance Innovation' is a proper name for an organization. #} {% trans cigi='https://www.cigionline.org/internet-survey-2016' %} Source: CIGI-Ipsos, 2016 {% endtrans %}

{{ _('What you can do about government surveillance') }}

  1. {% trans encryptedapps='http://www.greenbot.com/article/3119449/android/the-best-messaging-apps-with-end-to-end-encryption.html' %} Choose apps that keep your conversations encrypted. Here’s a list. {% endtrans %}
  2. {% trans strongpass='https://support.mozilla.org/kb/create-secure-passwords-keep-your-identity-safe?surveillance', passmanager='https://www.wired.com/2016/01/you-need-a-password-manager/?surveillance' %} Choose strong, unique pins and passwords, and use a password manager. (Note: we haven’t tried all the options on these lists – see what works for you.) {% endtrans %}

{{ _('What Mozilla is doing about government surveillance') }}

  1. {% trans reform='https://blog.mozilla.org/netpolicy/2016/09/19/improving-government-disclosure-of-security-vulnerabilities/' %} Recommending reform at the policy level to improve government disclosure of security vulnerabilities. {% endtrans %}
  2. {% trans usafreedom='https://blog.mozilla.org/netpolicy/2015/06/02/mozilla-applauds-u-s-senates-passage-of-the-usa-freedom-act/' %} Calling on lawmakers all over the globe to rein in mass surveillance, and helping to pass the USA Freedom Act. {% endtrans %}
  3. {% trans standford='http://cyberlaw.stanford.edu/events/government-hacking-rule-41' %} Cohosting talks with Stanford’s Center for Internet and Society about government hacking. {% endtrans %}
{# end #surveillance #}
